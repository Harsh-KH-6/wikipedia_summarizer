Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Wikipedia Summarizer collects minimal information necessary for its operation:

  • Search queries you enter
  • Feedback you provide (if you choose to do so)
  • Basic usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Wikipedia summaries
  • Improve our service
  • Respond to your feedback
  • Analyze usage patterns

3. Data Storage

Wikipedia Summarizer does not permanently store your search queries or personal information. All searches are processed in real-time and are not saved on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Wikipedia API for content
  • GitHub for issue tracking (if you choose to report issues)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of any data collection
  • Request information about how your data is used

6. Children's Privacy

Wikipedia Summarizer does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at projectfeedback06@gmail.com.

9. Last Updated
